## Title + Theme

The title of this website, <i>birdin.space</i>, was inspired by Constantin Brâncuși's [sculpture of the same name](https://www.guggenheim.org/artwork/669). Developed from Brâncuși's earlier set of Romanian [<i>Maiastra</i>](https://www.guggenheim.org/artwork/660) birds, the artwork intends to display the essence of flight, rather than depict the physical features of the bird itself. Being able to detail motion through art, however simple or complex it may be, has interested me for a long time. If you're also interested in this, I'd recommend taking a look at [<i>Dynamism of a Dog on a Leash</i>](https://www.albrightknox.org/artworks/196416-dinamismo-di-un-cane-al-guinzaglio-dynamism-dog-leash) by Giacomo Balla and [<i>The Red Horsemen</i>](https://www.wikiart.org/en/carlo-carra/the-red-horseman-1913) by Carlo Carrà, two prominent artists of the early 20th century Futurist art movement based in Italy<br><br>

The theme of this website aims to mimic the minimalism found within [Ubuntu](https://www.ubuntu.com) (a distribution of Linux OS). As a result, I made the color scheme and format match that found in the Ubuntu terminal.
